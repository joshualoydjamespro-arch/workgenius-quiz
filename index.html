Why Knowing Your Profile Changes Everything

Everyone has all six types to some degree, but you have two Geniuses where you thrive with natural energy and joy, two Competencies you can do well but that cost you energy, and two Frustrations that drain you significantly.

When you spend most of your time in your Geniuses, work feels fulfilling and sustainable. When you’re stuck in your Frustrations, burnout, disengagement, and frustration skyrocket — even if you’re “good” at the task.

The model’s power comes from teams: great teams have all six types represented. Knowing your profile (and others’) lets you play to strengths, delegate wisely, communicate needs, and build roles and teams where everyone thrives.

The Six Types of Working Genius

Created by Patrick Lencioni and The Table Group, this model identifies six distinct types of “genius” that people bring to work. Everyone possesses all six to varying degrees, but we each have two where we thrive with energy (Geniuses), two we can perform but that cost energy (Competencies), and two that significantly drain us (Frustrations).

WON Wonder — Curiosity, asking why, seeing possibilities and gaps.
INV Invention — Creating new ideas, solutions, and innovations.
DIS Discernment — Intuition, judgment, sensing what will work.
GAL Galvanizing — Rallying and inspiring others to action.
ENA Enablement — Supporting others and removing obstacles so they succeed.
TEN Tenacity — Pushing through, finishing, and driving to completion.

The goal: Spend more time in your Geniuses, protect yourself from Frustrations, and build teams where all six types are present and valued.
